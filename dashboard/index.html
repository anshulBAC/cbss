Auto-Handle Policy WHY IT'S SAFE
✅
LOW risk + FRESH context → Auto-Handle
Risk classifier confirms: no auth, payment, or cross-service blast radius. Context freshness verified: recent human review + low commit churn. Cumulative risk is bounded — faulty low-risk patches are isolated by design. Full audit trail written to audit_log.json — always reviewable.
🚦
HIGH risk or STALE context → Escalate
Any high-risk signal (auth, billing, cross-service) or stale context triggers mandatory human gates. Gate 1 validates the AI diagnosis (~30s). Gate 2 approves the patch with explicit accountability — no implicit consent. Rejection at either gate injects engineer context into the next AI pass.
